Dietary guidelines

  • ✔ Do not skip meals, as this will slow down your metabolism.
  • ✔ Reduce carbohydrate and sweet intake, especially sugar and sugary snacks.
  • ✔ Eat breakfast regularly to control hunger throughout the day.
  • ✔ Increase your intake of protein and fiber, such as fish, chicken, tofu, and fruits and vegetables.
  • ✔ Read nutrition labels and choose foods that are low in calories and fat.
  • ✔ Drink enough water, at least 1.5–2 liters per day.
  • ✔ Chew your food slowly to give your brain 15 minutes to register fullness.
